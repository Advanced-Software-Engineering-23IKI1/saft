# Projektstrukturplan
hier ist der Projektstrukturplan

```mermaid
flowchart TD

    A[Project Start] --> B[Requirements & Planning]
    B --> B1[Stakeholder Interviews]
    B --> B2[Define Scope]
    B --> B3[Timeline & Milestones]
    B --> B4[Tech Stack Decision]

    A --> C[Design Phase]
    C --> C1[UX Research]
    C --> C2[Wireframes]
    C --> C3[UI Design]
    C --> C4[Design Review]

    A --> D[Development Phase]
    D --> D1[Frontend Development]
    D --> D2[Backend Development]
    D --> D3[Database Setup]
    D --> D4[API Integration]

    D --> E[Testing Phase]
    E --> E1[Unit Testing]
    E --> E2[Integration Testing]
    E --> E3[User Acceptance Testing]
    E --> E4[Bug Fixing]

    E --> F[Deployment]
    F --> F1[Staging Environment]
    F --> F2[Production Release]
    F --> F3[Monitoring Setup]

    F --> G[Maintenance & Iteration]
    G --> G1[Performance Monitoring]
    G --> G2[User Feedback Collection]
    G --> G3[Feature Improvements]
    G --> G4[Security Updates]
```
